Hong Kong National Geopark covers an area of 50 square
kilometres across parts of the Eastern and Northeastern New
Territories.

Within the Sai Kung Volcanic Rock Region lie Sharp Island,
High Island, the Ninepin Group and the Ung Kong Group,
whereas the Northest New Territories Sedimentary Rock
Region includes

Double Haven
Tolo Channel
Tung Ping Chau
Port Island and Bluff Head
